Real-time document collaboration

Even we hate to read long text. We'll keep it short here.

Just a few things to consider -

  1. We do NOT read anyone's notes nor do we want to. We are cool like that.
  2. Your notes are safe on the URL as long as you want to. Keep 'em coming!
  3. The software runs on the WebSocket protocol introduced by HTML5. Hence users with old browsers may not be able to connect and collaborate with you.
  4. Since this is our side-project, there may be bugs in the code that we may not be aware of. Please be polite and draw our attention to them at livenote@hardik.co.

That's it, peace! - Aditya and Hardik.
